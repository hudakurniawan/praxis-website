Admissions

Join our innovative STEAM education program and start your journey to success

Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

Strong academic performance in previous studies with a minimum GPA of 3.0 and proficiency in core subjects.

Interest in STEAM

Demonstrated interest in Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics through projects or activities.

Personal Qualities

Strong motivation, creativity, teamwork skills, and a passion for learning and innovation.

Application Process

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form with all required documents including academic records, recommendation letters, and personal statement.

2

Assessment & Interview

Participate in academic assessment and personal interview to evaluate your fit for our STEAM program.

3

Portfolio Review

Submit a portfolio showcasing your projects, achievements, and creative work in STEAM-related areas.

4

Admission Decision

Receive admission decision within 2 weeks of completing all requirements. Successful candidates will be notified via email and phone.
