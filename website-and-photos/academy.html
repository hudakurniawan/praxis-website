Our Curriculum

Foundation Year (Grade 10)

  • Core Subjects (Mathematics, Science, Languages, Humanities)
  • STEAM Projects (Engineering, Art, Business, Science)
  • Self-Development Project

Specialization & Advanced Projects (Grade 11)

  • Advanced Core Subjects
  • Interdisciplinary Projects based on student interests
  • Internship at partner companies or universities

Higher Education Preparation (Grade 12)

  • Tutorials and test preparation for university admission
  • Capstone Social Project
  • University application guidance & counseling
