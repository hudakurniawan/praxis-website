About Praxis High School

Discover our mission, vision, and commitment to excellence in STEAM education

Our Mission

To empower students with innovative STEAM education, fostering creativity, critical thinking, and real-world problem-solving skills for the future.

Our Vision

To be the leading STEAM education institution in Yogyakarta, producing innovative thinkers and future leaders who make a positive impact on society.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Encouraging creative thinking and innovative approaches to problem-solving.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in education and personal development.

Collaboration

Fostering teamwork and cooperation among students and staff.

Our Journey

2020

Praxis High School was founded with a vision to revolutionize education in Yogyakarta.

2021

Launched our first STEAM programs and welcomed our inaugural class of students.

2022

Expanded our facilities and introduced advanced robotics and AI programs.

2023

Achieved recognition for excellence in STEAM education and student achievements.

2024

Continued growth and innovation, preparing students for the challenges of tomorrow.
