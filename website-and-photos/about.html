About Praxis High School

Discover our mission, vision, and commitment to excellence in STEAM education

Who We Are

Praxis High School is a pioneering institution in Yogyakarta committed to preparing students for the rapidly changing world of work. We focus on building 21st-century skills through a unique STEAM-based curriculum that integrates Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics.

Our Approach

We emphasize project-based learning, collaboration with industry mentors, and hands-on experiences. Students not only master academic knowledge but also develop critical thinking, creativity, and entrepreneurial skills essential for their future careers.

  • STEAM-integrated core curriculum
  • Real-world projects guided by mentors
  • Partnerships with universities and industries

The Future of Jobs

Based on the World Economic Forum’s Future of Jobs Report, 65% of students today will work in jobs that do not yet exist. At Praxis, we prepare our students to thrive in this uncertain future.

Our Vision

To become a leading educational institution that prepares students as future leaders with academic and professional competencies through Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics (STEAM)-based education.

Our Mission

We deliver future-oriented education by building students’ competencies in STEAM through projects in Engineering, Science, Art, and Business, providing opportunities to learn directly from experts, and partnering with various communities and institutions to enrich their learning experiences.
