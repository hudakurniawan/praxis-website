What is STEAM Education?

STEAM education integrates Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics into a cohesive learning paradigm. At Praxis High School, we believe that the arts are just as important as the sciences in developing well-rounded, creative thinkers who can solve complex problems and innovate for the future.

Our curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning, project-based education, and real-world applications. Students work on interdisciplinary projects that combine multiple STEAM disciplines, preparing them for the challenges and opportunities of the 21st century.
